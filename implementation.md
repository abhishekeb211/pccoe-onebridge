# Platform concept: **PCCOE OneBridge**

A unified **student success and inclusion platform** for **PCCOE** that supports students from **1st year to 4th year**, across **all branches**, while also integrating **Equal Opportunity Cell (EOC)** services, especially for **students with disabilities and disadvantaged groups**.

---

## 1. Core idea

**PCCOE OneBridge** would function as a **single digital ecosystem** where students can:

* raise academic and administrative requirements
* get support through a help desk
* discover scholarships and fellowships
* access internships and jobs year-wise
* use college facilities digitally
* receive EOC support, grievance help, and disability-friendly services

This makes the platform both a **student lifecycle platform** and an **inclusive opportunity platform**.

---

## 2. Problem it solves

At present, students usually face fragmented systems:

* academic requests are handled separately
* scholarships and fellowships are hard to track
* job opportunities are scattered
* support for disabled or disadvantaged students is not centralized
* access to facilities is manual or unclear

**OneBridge** solves this by bringing everything into **one accessible portal**.

## 4. Major platform modules

### A. Student Requirements Module

Students can submit and track:

* bonafide certificate requests
* ID card issues
* exam-related support
* fee-related concerns
* academic support requests
* lab or facility access requests

* disability accommodation requests

**Key features**

* status tracking: *submitted, under review, approved, resolved*

Use **smart routing** so requests automatically go to the correct department.

## 7. Accessibility-first design

This platform should be built with **universal design** principles.

**Must-have accessibility features**
* screen reader compatibility
* text-to-speech support
* large font mode
* high contrast mode
* keyboard-only navigation
* multilingual support
* simple interface for low digital literacy users
* audio and visual notifications
* accessible PDFs/forms
* sign-language/video guidance where possible

This is critical because the platform should not merely mention inclusion; it should be **structurally inclusive**.

## 13. One-paragraph concept note

**PCCOE OneBridge** is an integrated student support and opportunity platform designed for engineering students from first year to final year across all branches. It centralizes student requirements, academic and personal support, scholarships, fellowships, internships, jobs, and access to college facilities. By embedding Equal Opportunity Cell services, disability support, grievance redressal, and welfare guidance into the same ecosystem, the platform ensures that every student, especially those from disadvantaged or marginalized backgrounds, receives timely, inclusive, and career-focused support throughout their academic journey.
